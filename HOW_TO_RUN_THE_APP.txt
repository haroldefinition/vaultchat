nd===========================================================
 VAULTCHAT MOBILE APP — HOW TO SET UP
 No coding knowledge needed. Follow each step exactly.
===========================================================

WHAT THIS IS:
The mobile app that goes on people's iPhones and Android phones.
After following these steps you'll be able to run VaultChat
on your own phone and test it!

===========================================================
STEP 1 — Install Expo Go on your phone RIGHT NOW
===========================================================
This is a free app that lets you test VaultChat on your
phone WITHOUT needing to go through the App Store yet.

  iPhone: Search "Expo Go" in the App Store → Download
  Android: Search "Expo Go" in Google Play → Download

===========================================================
STEP 2 — Install Node.js on your computer (if not done)
===========================================================
  Go to: nodejs.org
  Click the big green LTS button → Download → Install

===========================================================
STEP 3 — Install the Expo tool on your computer
===========================================================
  1. On your computer open:
       Mac: Terminal (search "Terminal" in Spotlight)
       Windows: Command Prompt (search "cmd")

  2. Type this EXACTLY and press Enter:
       npm install -g expo-cli

  3. Wait for it to finish (takes 2-3 minutes)

===========================================================
STEP 4 — Put the app files on your computer
===========================================================
  1. Create a new folder on your Desktop called "VaultChat"
  2. Put ALL the files Claude gave you into that folder:
       - App.js
       - app.json
       - package.json
       - The "src" folder with all its contents

===========================================================
STEP 5 — Install app dependencies
===========================================================
  1. In Terminal/Command Prompt, type:
       cd Desktop/VaultChat
     Then press Enter

  2. Then type:
       npm install
     Then press Enter

  3. Wait — this downloads everything needed (2-5 minutes)
     You'll see a lot of text scrolling — that's normal!

===========================================================
STEP 6 — Start the app
===========================================================
  1. Type this and press Enter:
       npx expo start

  2. A QR code will appear in the Terminal window

===========================================================
STEP 7 — Open on your phone
===========================================================
  iPhone:
    1. Open your iPhone Camera app
    2. Point it at the QR code on your computer screen
    3. Tap the notification that appears
    4. VaultChat opens in Expo Go!

  Android:
    1. Open the Expo Go app you downloaded
    2. Tap "Scan QR Code"
    3. Point at the QR code
    4. VaultChat opens!

===========================================================
 YOU SHOULD NOW SEE VAULTCHAT RUNNING ON YOUR PHONE!
===========================================================

===========================================================
STEP 8 — When ready for the real App Store
===========================================================
When you're happy with how it looks and works, come back
to Claude and say "I'm ready to submit to the App Store"

Claude will then guide you through:
  - Creating your Apple Developer account ($99)
  - Submitting to the iOS App Store
  - Creating your Google Play account ($25)
  - Submitting to Android Google Play

===========================================================
IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG
===========================================================
Just screenshot whatever you see and show Claude.
Claude will fix it immediately. Nothing is unfixable.

Common issues:
  - "command not found" → Node.js wasn't installed, redo Step 2
  - QR code doesn't work → make sure phone and computer
    are on the same WiFi network
  - App crashes → screenshot it and show Claude
